# ✨ Offer Box Redesign - Course Cards

## 🎨 What Changed:

### Before:
- ❌ Large white box with thick green border
- ❌ Too prominent and blocky
- ❌ Took up too much space
- ❌ Center-aligned text (didn't match card flow)

### After:
- ✅ Sleek, elegant badge-style design
- ✅ Semi-transparent background with blur effect
- ✅ Subtle border and soft shadows
- ✅ Right-aligned text (natural reading flow)
- ✅ Gradient backgrounds on labels
- ✅ More compact and integrated

---

## 🎯 Design Improvements:

### 1. **Background**
- Semi-transparent white with blur effect (backdrop-filter)
- Soft gradient overlay
- Makes it feel lighter and more modern

### 2. **Border & Shadow**
- Reduced border width: 2px → 1.5px
- Made border semi-transparent (30% opacity)
- Added layered shadows for depth
- Soft green glow effect

### 3. **"OFFER PRICE" Label**
- Now has gradient background (dark green to lighter green)
- Stronger font weight (700 → 800)
- Better shadow effect
- More rounded corners

### 4. **Discount Badge**
- Added gradient background (light green shades)
- Added subtle border
- Changed to inline-block for better spacing
- More compact padding

### 5. **Price Display**
- Changed font from Playfair to DM Sans (matches card better)
- Slightly smaller but bolder
- Better letter spacing
- Changed alignment to right (natural flow)

### 6. **Positioning**
- Moved slightly down (2.2rem → 2.4rem from top)
- Reduced minimum width (100px → 80px)
- More breathing room

---

## 📱 Mobile Responsive:

- Even more compact on mobile
- Maintains legibility
- Scales proportionally
- All text remains crisp

---

## 🎨 Visual Hierarchy:

1. **Badge** - Catches eye first (bright green gradient)
2. **Discount %** - Secondary attention (light green badge)
3. **Old Price** - Subtle (gray, strikethrough)
4. **New Price** - Main focus (large, bold green)

---

## ✅ Result:

The offer box now feels like a **natural part of the card** rather than an overlaid element. It's elegant, modern, and doesn't overwhelm the course information!

Perfect balance between:
- Being noticeable 👁️
- Not being intrusive 🎯
- Matching the card aesthetic ✨
